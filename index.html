ABOUT

The Hamel Hawks are a town ball team based in Hamel, Minnesota, a charming small-town gem just west of the Twin Cities along Highway 55. Perfectly positioned near Maple Grove, Plymouth, Minnetonka, Medina, and Minneapolis, Hamel offers a central location that's easy to reach while preserving its tight-knit community vibe. As a summer baseball team in Minnesota, the Hamel Hawks draw talent from across the west metro area, making us a local favorite for fans and players alike.

Our roster showcases a dynamic mix of skill and passion, featuring Division 1 athletes from nearby colleges and seasoned veterans who live for the crack of the bat. The Hawks are committed to developing young players, playing baseball the right way, and building a sense of pride for Hamel and surrounding towns like Medina and Maple Grove. We've poured heart into upgrading Paul Fortin Memorial Field, creating a top-notch experience for players and fans. From better seating to improved facilities, these enhancements make every game a must-see event for families and baseball fans. Read about upgrades.

*New in 2025!* The Hamel Hawks now compete as a Class B team in the North Star League, bringing exciting summer baseball to the west metro. Our 16-game league schedule includes two matchups against each of the other five Class B teams and one game against each Class C opponent. Catch the action at Paul Fortin Memorial Field in Hamel — check out the 2025 schedule and plan your visit from Maple Grove, Medina, or beyond. Whether you're a local from Hamel or a fan making the short trip from Minneapolis, join us for a summer of baseball that celebrates community and competition!
