ABOUT

The Hamel Hawks are an amateur baseball team founded in 1926. Located just west of the Twin Cities off of highway 55, Hamel is centrally located but still has the traditional town ball feel. The Hawks are a summer baseball team in Minnesota for players that live near the west metro area. The talent level on our team ranges from Division 1 to older guys who just want to keep playing. Hamel has a proud tradition, winning the 1987 and 1997 Class B State Tournament. Manager Tim Flemming has been a part of Hamel Baseball for over 40 years. The Hawks have made several renovations to Paul Fortin Memorial Field just in time for the 2024 season. Read about those upgrades here.
