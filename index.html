ABOUT

The Hamel Hawks are a town ball team in Hamel, Minnesota. Nestled just west of the Twin Cities along Highway 55, Hamel offers a central location while maintaining its charming small-town atmosphere. As a summer baseball team in Minnesota, the Hamel Hawks attract players from the west metro area. Our roster boasts a diverse range of talent, from Division 1 athletes to seasoned veterans who simply love the game. Under the guidance of Manager Tim Flemming, a dedicated figure with over 40 years of involvement in Hamel baseball, the team continues to prioritize playing the game the right way and fostering a sense of community within the town. In preparation for the 2024 season, the Hawks have invested in several upgrades to Paul Fortin Memorial Field, enhancing the experience for both players and fans. Read about those upgrades here.
